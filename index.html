Why Software Testing Matters

"Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of intelligent effort." John Ruskin

Software Testing should never be an after-thought. There are many companies that have rushed to ship software and had the biggest regrets of their time.

The industry is changing at a rapid pace. With a host of new gadgets and devices, businesses are struggling to keep up with supporting multiple platforms and form-factors.

My skillset as a tester, is my ability to ensure that applications have a consistent user experience across all the various platforms. This creates excitement for users as well as ensure that quality is not compromised.

With the rapid adoption of HTML5, this is creating a uniqe set of opportunities for Businesses as well as a set of challenges. Backwards compatibility with older browsers is one of these challenges. I'm uniquely skilled in ensuring that older browsers can still render the application usable and functional, even if without the bells and whistles of HTML5.

Whether it's mobile applications, tablet applications or HTML5 based applications, I strive for quality, digging and looking for the most obscure bugs.

You wanna catch your bugs before you ship? That's where I come in! :)
